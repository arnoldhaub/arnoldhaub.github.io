More about myself.

I am currently following a training to become a web developper at BeCode, in the center of Brussels.

Before starting this training, I did a bachelor degree in eBusiness. I did not like working as a marketer which made me think a lot about what I really wanted to do with my life. After a long time of reflection, I realized that I have always been attracted to code. Solving problems, creating projects, and constantly learning are things I like to do on a daily basis and that is, in a certain way, what a developper does. It became clear to me that I wanted to try and see where it could take me, and here I am.

Outside of coding, I love to write and read. I have always been a dreamer, and writing and reading help me develop my imagination and create stories. I love the process of having an idea in my head and trying to bring it to life by writing it down and letting my inspiration do the rest of the work.
